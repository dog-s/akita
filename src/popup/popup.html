Tired of Ads?

Ads might be annoying and come at the cost of your personal privacy, but they let you browse the web for free. With Web Monetization you can:

  • Access website content
  • Avoid annoying ads altogether
  • Keep your personal data private
  • Support the website

What is Web Monetization?

Web Monetization allows you to stream micropayments (send tiny payments over time) directly to websites. Websites can detect these payments and turn off ads, as well as further enhance your website experience.
